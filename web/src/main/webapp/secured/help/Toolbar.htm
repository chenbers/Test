Toolbar

The toolbar appears at the top of every page. From here, you can access help files, your account information through the My Account link, or log out.

It is found in the top right corner of every page.

Clicking on the Help button will open a help page for that particular screen. For example, if you are in the Admin section, looking at your Devices page, clicking the Help icon will display a page about the admin portion of your devices.

My Account will help you set up your personal information.

Logout will log you out of the website.
