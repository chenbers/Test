This lists all the warnings associated with your group. You can sort ascending or descending by:

  • Warning

  • Vehicle

  • Driver

  • Date/Time

In the far right column is a link to the details of the event. Here, you'll have details for:

  • Date/Time

  • Group

  • Driver

  • Vehicle

  • Category

  • Detail

 

 

Clicking Edit Columns above the directional arrows takes you to a screen that allows you to select and deselect your desired columns.
