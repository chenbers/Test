At every 5-mile increment, you can set the over-the-limit setting. For example, let's say you want a 10% grace area for your drivers for everything over 30 MPH. You can have speed notifications for 0 MPH over the limit for 5, 10, 15, 20, and 25, 3 MPH overthe limit for 30 and 35, 4 MPH over the limit for 40 and 45, 5 MPH over the limit for  50 and 55, etc.

 

The maximum over-the-limit setting is 25 MPH.

 

The point of this is to allow your drivers the flexibility YOU feel is acceptable.
