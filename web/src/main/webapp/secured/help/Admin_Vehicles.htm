Vehicles

This is a page of all your vehicles. You can sort the columns by Vehicle ID, Driver, VIN, or Status. To edit information about a person, click Edit  in the far right column.

From here, you can add a vehicle to your fleet by clicking Add Vehicle under the Vehicles tab.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
