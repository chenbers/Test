Drivers

This page displays a report of your drivers:

  • which group they're in

  • employee ID

  • employee name

  • the vehicle being driven

  • miles driven

  • overall score

  • speed, style and seat belt scores.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.

You can search for records by name or vehicle, edit columns, sort the report by team, Employee ID, Employee, Vehicle, or Miles Driven. To do so, click on on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by descending order. Clicking twice will sort by ascending order.

Right below the Red Flags notifications, you'll see an Edit Columns link. Click this to edit the columns that appear on the Driver Safety Report.

When you click on a particular Driver's name or on any given score in their row, you are taken to a more detailed page with respect to their driving record.
