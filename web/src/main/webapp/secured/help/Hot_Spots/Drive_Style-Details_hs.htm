The Details section lists where, when and the severity of an aggressive driving event/behavior. Click an address in the Location column to see where an event occurred.

 

If warranted, click Clear to remove a recorded event.

 

**NOTE**

Upon clearing a recorded event, the driver's overall driving style score and their score for a particular behavior will not be immediately adjusted. The scores will be adjusted once the system performs a recalculation of such scores--generally, once an hour.

 

You can sort the events by ascending or descending (default) order for any of the listed columns:

  • Location

  • Date/Time

  • Event

  • Speed

  • Severity

You can also forgive any event by clicking Clear in the far right column.
