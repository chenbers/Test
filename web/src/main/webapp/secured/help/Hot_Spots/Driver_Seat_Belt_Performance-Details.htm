The details section lists where and when a recorded seat belt violation occurred.

 

Click and address in the Location column to see where the violation occured.

 

If warranted, click Clear to remove a recorded violation.

 

**NOTE**

Upon clearing a recorded event, the driver's overall seat belt score will not be immediately adjusted. The scores will be adjusted once the system performs a recalculation of such scores--generally, once an hour.

 

 

You can sort the events by ascending or descending (default) order for any of the listed columns:

  • Location

  • Date/Time

  • Avg Speed

  • Top Speed

  • Distance
