People

This is a page of all your fleet. You can sort the columns by Employee ID, Employee, User Status, or Group. To edit information about a person, click Edit  in the far right column.

From here, you can add a person to your fleet by clicking Add Person under the People tab.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
