Red Flags

This page displays a list of your fleet's red flags. A "red flag" is considered anything  unsafe: hard turn, hard break, speeding, seat belt violation.

You can search for records by name or vehicle, edit columns, sort the report by level, alerts, or groups. To sort columns, click on the column title. Clicking once will sort by descending order. Clicking twice will sort by ascending order.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.

The columns on the page are editable. Click the Edit Columns button. A window will open.

The default columns are already selected. Once you are happy with what columns are selected, click Save. You will be taken back to the Red Flags screen with your new columns settings displayed. If you do not want to save your changes, click Cancel. You will be taken back to the Red Flags screen, but it will have the same columns as before.
