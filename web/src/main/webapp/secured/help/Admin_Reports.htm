Reports

On this page, you can see a list of your reports. A report consists of a reporting schedule (name, status, time of day, and occurrance), report settings (type of report, group), and the email address to where the report is being sent.

Reports are automatically generated at the specified time, either on a daily, weekly, or montly schedule.
