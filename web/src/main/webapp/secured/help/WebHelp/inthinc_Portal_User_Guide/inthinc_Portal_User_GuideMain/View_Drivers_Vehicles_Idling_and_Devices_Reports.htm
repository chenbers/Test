View Drivers, Vehicles, Idling, and Devices Reports

1.   Go to Reports page.

2.   Click Drivers or Idling to view driver-specific reports, or click a link to view a specific group or individual reports.

3.   Click Vehicles or Devices to view report related to specific vehicles or devices. Most reports have links to individual reports.

These reports display a numeric score(0.0 to 5.0) for the previous 12 months. The system calculates this score using a formula that takes into account speeding, seat belt violations, aggressive driving behaviors, and the number of miles driven. The system displays the number of driving events and miles driven under each time period, providing a general sense of how this score was calculated.
