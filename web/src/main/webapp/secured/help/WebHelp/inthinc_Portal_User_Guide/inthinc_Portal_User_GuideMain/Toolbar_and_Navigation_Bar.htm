Toolbar and Navigation Bar

Near the top of each inthinc® Portal page is a toolbar and a navigation bar (Figure 3).

The Toolbar appears at the top right corner of every page of the portal (highlighted in green in Figure 3). From here, you can access help files, view your account information, or log out of the Web site.

•   Help will open a help page for that particular screen. For example, if you are in the Admin section, looking at your Devices page, clicking the Help icon will display a page about the admin portion of your devices.

•   My Messages allows you to read and compose text messages and to review messages sent.

•   My Account will help you set up your personal information.

•   Log Out will log you out of the Web site.

The Navigation Bar (highlighted in red in Figure 3) has six tabs and a Search field and filter. These features allow you to move easily among the portal pages Home, Reports, Notifications, Live Fleet, HOS, and Admin, or help you locate specific portal information.
