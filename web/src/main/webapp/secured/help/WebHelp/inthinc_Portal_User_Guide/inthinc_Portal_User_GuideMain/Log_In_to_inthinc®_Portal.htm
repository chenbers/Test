Log In to inthinc® Portal

Your inthinc® Portal account will be activated the first time you log in. To log in:

1.   Go to the inthinc® Portal web site at http://my.stage.inthinc.com.
2.   Enter the username and password assigned to you by your fleet manager, then click Log In. The inthinc® Portal interface opens with the Home page selected.
3.   If you have forgotten your password, click Forgot your username or password? below the login button. A window opens, asking for your email address. Enter your address.
