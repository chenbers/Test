Portal Overview

These instructions are designed to guide you in the use of the inthinc® portal (available at http://my.inthinc.com or at your company-specific site, such as http://acme.inthinc.com)—a system that provides communications regarding driving speeds and behaviors of your fleet drivers. These communications can help you increase driver education and safety. Drivers can be warned immedi­ately with verbal and visual message about unsafe behaviors. Management can be alerted to driving behaviors by e-mail or text message. Management can also access detailed information on team and individual driving behaviors through the inthinc® portal.
