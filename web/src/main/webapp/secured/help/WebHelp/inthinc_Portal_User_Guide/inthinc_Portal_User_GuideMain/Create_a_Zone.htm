Create a Zone

Zones allow you to draw specific geographical boundaries (Figure 39 on page 39). Once you have created zones from the Zones tab, you can set up Zone Alerts from the Red Flags tab.

Note: Zone Alerts are “Information” type of notifications. To specify how a user is notified for each type of notification (Information, Warning, or Critical), edit the users page (see "Update a User" on page 31). There you can choose whether the user receives an e-mail, a text message, a phone call, or no notification at all.

1.   Go to Admin page > Zones tab.

Figure 39: Zone page

2.   Click Add Zone (upper-right). The Add Zone form displays (Figure 40 on page 40).

 

Figure 40: Add Zone form

3.   Enter a Zone name in the field provided.

4.   Choose a type of view (drop-down list in upper-right of map):

•   Map view shows street names. (This is the default view.)

•   Satellite view is an aerial photograph.

•   Hybrid view is the Map view superimposed on the Satellite view.

5.   Use the map’s arrow buttons and zoom slider (on the left) to bring the desired location into view. Or use (the grabber hand) or the Find Address box at the top.

Tip: You can also use (the center arrow button) to return to the point of origin.

6.   Set Zone parameters: Click on one of the shape tools at top of map to choose a starting shape for your zone, then select the zone area on the map as described below.

Square – On the map, click where you want the upper left corner of the zone to be then drag-and-drop to the lower right corner. The square zone appears. If the zone is incorrect, click on the edge of the shape to display points, then click-an-drag a point to reposition it.

Circle – On the map, click where you want the center of the circle to be and then click where you want the edge of the circle to be. The circular zone appears from the center point around to the edge point. Click the circle to enlarge the area, if desired. If the zone is incorrect, click on the edge of the shape to display points, then click-and-drag a point to reposition it.

Polygon – On the map, click the first point of the polygon, then click the next point. Continue clicking around the area you wish to define. Once the area is defined, click on the starting point to close the zone shape. If you need to adjust the shape of the polygon, click on the edge of the shape to display points, then click-and-drag a point to reposition it.

7.   Set violation monitoring options on the right side of the Add Zone form. You can set any of these monitors to Off within a given zone for all vehicle types or for a selected vehicle type (light or heavy duty).

8.   Click Save Zone. The new zone now displays in the drop-down list on the Zones page.

9.   (Optional) To download the zone to a thumb drive (to install zone on devices that are out of cellular or satellite range), click the Download link on the Zone page and follow the on-screen prompts.
