View Notifications

View notifications by notification type (Red Flags, Safety, Diagnostics, Zones, HOS, Emergency, or Crash History) to review compliance to the preferences defined in your system. You can set several parameters to filter notifications and immediately refresh the screen and narrow the results.

To view any of these notifications:

1.   Go to the Notifications page and choose a Notifications type: Red Flags, Safety, Diagnostics, Zones, HOS, Emergency, or Crash History.

Figure 53: Notifications options

2.   Choose a Team.

3.   Choose the Time Frame (any given day in the past week, the entire past week, last 30 days, past month, or past year).

4.   Click Refresh. The notification list requested displays.

5.   (Optional) Use one or more filters to further narrow your search results. These vary per notification type. In the Red Flags example shown in Figure 53, the additional filters are Level and Category.

•   Level indicates level of severity: Critical, Warning, or Information.

•   Category: Available for most types of notifications, but category drop-down menu varies per notification type (see the Red Flags cate­gory options in Figure 54).

6.   (Optional) Choose a sort option: Columns with arrows can be sorted in descending or ascending order by clicking the column heading once or twice.

7.   View Alert Details: Click the link in the column provided and follow the online prompts.

8.   (Optional) Export the report: The data found in these reports can be exported to email, PDF, or Excel format by clicking the utility icon on the upper-right side of the report and completing the dialog box.

9.   (Optional) Customize the display by using the Edit Columns link (this is described in detail in the next section.
