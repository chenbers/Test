Compose a waySmart™ Text Message

Use these instructions to compose a text message to send to your waySmart™ drivers, vehicles, or groups. 

1.   In the toolbar at the top of the page, click My Messages. Your Inbox tab is selected by default.
2.   Click Compose tab (on the right). The Compose Message page displays (see Figure 50).
3.   Create a distribution list for this message:
a.   Highlight one or more Drivers, Vehicles, or Groups, and then click the right arrow to add the selected items to the list. Note: To highlight more than one item at a time, press and hold the CTRL button.
b.   Click the right arrow to move selected items to the distribution box.
3.   Click in the Compose Message box and compose your message OR copy and paste a message into the box.
4.   Click Send.
