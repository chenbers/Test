Log In to inthinc Portal

Your tiwiPro™ Portal  account will be activated the first time you log in. To log in:

1.   Go to the tiwiPro™ Portal web site (http://my.inthinc.com) or your company-specific site (such as http://acme.inthinc.com).

2.   Enter the username and password assigned to you by your fleet manager, then click Log In. The tiwiPro™ Portal  interface opens with your Home page selected. Home page layouts vary, depending on your role in your organiza­tion. For examples of types of Home pages, see "Home Pages" on page 9.

3.   If you have forgotten your password:

a.   Click Forgot your username or password? below the login button.

A window opens, asking for your email address.

b.   Enter your address. A message will be sent to help you resolve the issues.
