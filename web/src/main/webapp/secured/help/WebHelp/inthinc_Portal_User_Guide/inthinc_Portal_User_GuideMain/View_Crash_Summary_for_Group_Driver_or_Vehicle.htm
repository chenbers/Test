View Crash Summary for Group, Driver, or Vehicle

Note: The Fleet, Division, and Team home pages, which display for Admins over those groups, include group-specific crash summaries at the top of the applicable Home page. Likewise, users with Admin rights over an individual Driver or Vehicle can view the crash summary for that driver or vehicle at the top of the individual driver or vehicle Home page.

1.   Log in to the tiwiPro™ Portal portal as Admin.

2.   When your Home page displays, view the Crash Summary for your Fleet, Division, Team, Driver or Vehicle at the top of the page. The summary includes these crash data points.

•   Crashes per million miles

•   Days since last crash

•   Total distance driven since last crash

Crashes Per Million Miles and Miles Since Last Crash are calculated over the past 12 months, or for however long data is available if less. Days Since Last Crash displays the number of days since last crash for all drivers in the hierarchy.

Fleet managers will see instantly how well everyone in the company is performing regarding crashes.

Division managers will have instant access to their jurisdiction drivers’ data. Data can be compared between sub-divi­sions and teams to identify trouble spots.
