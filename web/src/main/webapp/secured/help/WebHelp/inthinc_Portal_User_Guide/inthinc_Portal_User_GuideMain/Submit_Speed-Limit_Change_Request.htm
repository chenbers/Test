Submit Speed-Limit Change Request

At http://www.stage.inthinc.com, we’ve set up an easy way to for you to notify us if our speed limit data needs to be updated. Our Speed by Street team reviews the information provided on a regular basis, then updates our database as needed.

SPEED LIMIT DATA IS NOT EXACT. Speed-by-Street covers millions of streets in the United States. While the information from your inthinc® device is the most accurate available, we recognize that there will be instances when this data needs to be updated. YOU, as a part of the inthinc® community, have a hand in making this information better.

Note: This service is not available in every country. If your vehicle is driven outside of the countries we support, then the unique Speed-by-Street safety feature of your inthinc™ device will not be available.

1.   Under Admin, click the Speed by Street tab. The Speed by Street Change Request screen (Figure 46) appears.

Figure 46: Speed by Street Change Request form

2.   In the map, select the road and/or sections of road you feel need to be modified.

Note: To find your area faster, you can type an address in the Search field

3.   When you are satisfied with your change requests, click Submit Request above the map.

Thank you for your assistance in improving the inthinc® Speed-by-Street experience.
