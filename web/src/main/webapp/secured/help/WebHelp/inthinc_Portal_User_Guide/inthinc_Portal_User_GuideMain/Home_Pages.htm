Home Pages

A Home page is selected for you based on your assigned role(s) in the organiza­tion. Depending on the level of access granted to your login user account, the Home page displayed provides access to your fleet, region, team, or individual driving results—the highest level of your area of responsibility. For example:

Figure 2 on page 10 shows the fleet view that displays for a fleet manager or regional fleet (division) manager.

Figure 3 on page 10 shows a team view that displays for a team manager.

Figure  on page 15 shows an individual driver performance page that displays when an individual driver logs in or when the manager chooses to view information on the individual driver.

Figure  on page 16 shows an individual vehicle performance page that displays when an individual vehicle is selected.

Fleet and Regional Manager Home Page

The home page for fleet and regional managers displays:

•   Overall score pie chart

•   Scoring trends line chart per team or division

•   Miles-per-gallon bar chart

•   Divisions or teams list, depending on the access level

•   Crash statistics

•   Speeding percentage

•   Idling percentage

•   Live Fleet current locations map

The regional/division manager’s page dis­plays a fleet map of all vehicles in the group.

Team Manager Home Page

Team Manager Home Page is the Driver Statistics view of the Team Page. When a team manager logs in, the default Driver Statistics view displays. The manager can view team information in a variety of formats by clicking on the differ­ent tabs provided. Details about the different Team views is available in the "Team Page" section that follows.

The team’s position in the company hierarchy is displayed near the top of the page (highlighted in red). Each item in the path also serves as a shortcut to any team or division pages in the path. For example, if you were on the page shown in Figure 3, and clicked on the word “ASkip’s Division,” the page would refresh to display that divisions team page (as long as you have rights to that information).

To customize the display, choose Edit Columns link. To export the display, use the Export utility. (Both if these are highlighted in green.)
