Home Pages

A Home page is selected for you based on your assigned role(s) in the organiza­tion. Depending on the level of access granted to your login user account, the Home page displayed provides access to your fleet, region, team, or individual driving results—the highest level of your area of responsibility. For example:

Figure 2 on page 9 shows the fleet view that displays for a fleet manager or regional fleet (division) manager.

Figure 3 on page 10 shows a team view that displays for a team manager.

Figure 12 on page 15 shows an individ­ual driver performance page that dis­plays when an individual driver logs in or when the manager chooses to view infor­mation on the individual driver.

Fleet and Regional Manager Home Page

The home page for fleet and regional managers displays a pie chart of overall score, a line chart displaying scoring trends per team or division, and a miles-per-gallon bar chart, and a list of divi­sions or teams, depending on the access level.

The regional or division manager’s page displays a fleet map of all vehicles in the group.

Team Manager Home Page

Team Manager Home Page is the Driver Statistics view of the Team Page. When a team manager logs in, the default Driver Statistics view displays. The manager can view team information in a variety of formats by clicking on the differ­ent tabs provided. Details about the different Team views is available in the "Team Page" section that follows.

The team’s position in the company hierarchy is displayed near the top of the page. Each item in the path also serves as a shortcut to any team or division pages included in the path. For example, if you were on the page shown in Figure 3, and clicked on the word “ASkip’s Division,” the page would refresh to display that divisions team page (as long as you have rights to that information).
