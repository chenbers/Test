Maintain Your Account Profile

You can maintain your account information as needed online through the inthinc® Portal.

1.   At the top of the screen, click My Account link (Figure 1).

 

 Figure 1:  Access My Account Information
2.   The My Account screen appears (Figure 2), with account information, notifications, login information, and contact information.


 Figure 2: My Account Page


3.   If you need to change your password, click Change Password, make the change, then click Change. 


4.   Choose a password for your account. For security reasons, the best password is between 7-12 characters, using both letters and numbers. In the event that your password is lost, contact a Customer Service Representative at 1-866-294-8637 option 3.


5.   If you need to update your account, click the Edit button and complete the form. Enter Contact Information, then select your Notification options. Use the following to learn more about these options and to specify your own pref­erences.

 
Enter your preferred email address, phone number and text message address that you would like used to notify you when your driver arrives or leaves a specific zone that you set up.

 
Set the primary email address that you would like to use for your inthinc® Portal account. This email address will be used for all notification purposes, including red flags, alerts and customer service.

 
The primary email address information you enter will be the default method of communication from inthinc® Portal.

 
Enter up to two text message addresses. Your text message address is your mobile phone number@your wireless ser­vice provider address. For example, if your mobile number is (555) 123-4567 and your service provider is T-Mobile, your text message address would be 5551234567@tmomail.net.

6.   Set Red Flag Preferences. After entering your Contact Information, choose your notification preferences regarding Information, Warning, and Critical notifications by selecting an item in each drop-down list in the Notifications sec­tion. For example, you may choose not to be notified of anything non-critical (i.e. Zone notifications) and only receive a phone call or text message for the most severe safety violations.
