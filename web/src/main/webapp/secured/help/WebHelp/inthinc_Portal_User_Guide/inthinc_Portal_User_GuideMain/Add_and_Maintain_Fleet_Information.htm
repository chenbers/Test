Add and Maintain Fleet Information

Add information regarding vehicles, inthinc® devices, and users to the system and associate the information as appropri­ate. Users can be granted portal login access only, be identified as a driver only, or they can have both login access and be identified as a driver. As inthinc™ devices, vehicles, and drivers are added to the system, the associations grow - devices with vehicles, vehicles with drivers, users with teams, etc.

Tip: Prior to adding users to the system, create an organization hierarchy made up of divisions and teams (groups) AND add as many custom roles as needed to identify your various login needs. Having the organizational hierarchy and roles defined early helps you avoid rework on user objects later.
