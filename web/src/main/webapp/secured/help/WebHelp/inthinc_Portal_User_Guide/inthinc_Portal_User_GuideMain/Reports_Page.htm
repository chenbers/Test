Reports Page

When you choose the Reports page in the Navigation Bar, the Drivers Report page (Figure 13) displays by default.

Types of Reports: By clicking one of the options just below the main navigation bar, you can view reports for Drivers, Vehicles, Idling, or Devices. If you have a waySmart™ device, you can also access waySmart™ report options from here. These reports (1) provide group summaries in spreadsheet format and (2) provide access to individual reports on sub-groups, drivers, or vehicles (both active and idling).

Sort Options: Reports can be sorted by a variety of columns. Arrows in column headings indicate additional sort options: Clicking any of the column headings once will reverse the sort order to either an ascending or descending order.

Export Options: Depending on the report, data found in these reports can be exported as Table, HTML page, email, PDF file, or Excel file by clicking the utility icon on the upper-right side of the report, or by choosing one of the delivery options presented in a waySmart™ report page.
