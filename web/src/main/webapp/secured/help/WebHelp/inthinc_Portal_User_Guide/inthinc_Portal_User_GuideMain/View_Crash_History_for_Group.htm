View Crash History for Group

Note: In order to view the Crash History page for your team, division, or fleet, your division manager or fleet manager has to have given you access to this list.

To view the Crash History list:

1.   Log in to tiwiPro™ Portal  portal.

2.   From the Home page menu, select Notification > Crash History. The Crash History page is displayed.

Figure 61: Crash History page

3.   Choose a Team in the drop-down list.

4.  To reload the Crash History data after choosing a team, do one of the following:

•   Internet Explorer users: Click Refresh at the top of the page.

•   Firefox users: Right-click on the page and choose Reload/Refresh.

5.   To export this history to email, PDF, or Excel format, click the Export utility icon (upper-right) and complete the dialog box.
