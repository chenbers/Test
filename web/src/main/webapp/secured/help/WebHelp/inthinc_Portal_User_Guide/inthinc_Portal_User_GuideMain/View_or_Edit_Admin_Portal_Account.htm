View or Edit Admin Portal Account

To view the Admin portal account for your company, go to Admin page > Account tab. The settings on this page deter­mine many company-specific displays found in the tiwiPro™ Portal, such as how to display an Unknown Driver and where the system can access your company maps that you want to overlay onto Google Maps.   

Note: This is NOT your personal account. For your personal account, click My Account (see Figure 1 on page 6).

Figure 37: Account Details page

To edit these company-wide Account settings, click Edit (upper-right). Use the descriptions that follow to help you com­plete the screen. Fields marked with a red asterisk are required. When you are finished making changes, click Save.

Unknown Driver: Set a name (such as Unknown Driver) that will be displayed when no driver is assigned or logged in.

Miscellaneous:

Phone Alerts Active: Choose whether or not to activate phone alerts.

No Reply Email: Provide an email address from your company to be used by the portal for sending no-reply E-mails.

Address: Your company address.

Web Map Service: The web mapping service allows you to display your company’s spatial data on top of the Google maps. To do this, you must define the following fields:

URL = Address to your map server

Query = The tabular/label data you want to display.

Layers = The data you want to show.

Query Parameters = The tabular/label data you want to display with a filter.

Support Contact Information: Your company’s tiwiPro™ Portal support contact information. While five fields are pro­vided, only the fields with content will display once updates are saved. For formatting examples of Support Contact Information (created in a web-ready format), click the Example button on the right of the screen.
