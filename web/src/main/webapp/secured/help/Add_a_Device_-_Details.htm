Add a Device - Details

The first screen you will see is the Details tab. Use this to populate the data about the new device.

To assign your device to a vehicle, click Assign under Device Management. The following dialog opens:

Scroll through the vehicles using the navigation bar at the bottom of the dialog. When you've found the vehicle to which you would like to assign the device, click Assign in the far right column. The device will be assigned to that particular vehicle.
