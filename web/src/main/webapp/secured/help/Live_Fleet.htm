Live Fleet

When you click Live Fleet, you are taken to a page with a map. A Dispatch window appears on the left side, listing your current drivers.

From here, you have the option of typing an address to locate your vehicles. Once you do this, you are taken to the location requested.  

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
