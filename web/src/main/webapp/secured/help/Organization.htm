Organization

This page displays a list of your organization's assets. From here, you can change your default map on the Live Fleet page.

To change the default map, perform the following:

  1. In the top right, click Edit.

The map will change to the following:

The fundamental differences are that you now have the ability to zoom in and out of the map, using the slider on the left side. Also, the summary data at the top is editable.

  1. If desired, modify the data in the summary fields as desired.

  2. To set the desired location, perform the following:

    1. In the Find Address field, type the desired address, then click Locate

      OR

    2. Use the slider to zoom in and out, then click and drag the map to find your desired location.

  3. When you've located your desired default location, click Save. Your default location will appear on the Live Fleet page.
