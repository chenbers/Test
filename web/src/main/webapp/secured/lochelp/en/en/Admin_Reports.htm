Reports

On this page, you can see a list of your reports. A report consists of a reporting schedule (name, status, time of day, and occurrence), report settings (type of report, group), and the e-mail address to where the report is being sent.

Reports are automatically generated at the specified time, either on a daily, weekly, or monthly schedule.
