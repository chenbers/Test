Overview

When you log into the portal, the first page you see is the Overview page. Fleet managers have access to this page. This page displays your fleet's overall score and trend, individual divisions' scores, crash statistics, speeding percentages, idling percentages, live fleet map and fuel efficiency.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
