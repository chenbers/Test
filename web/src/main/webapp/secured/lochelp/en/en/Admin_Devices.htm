Devices

This page is a listing of all devices in your fleet. You can search for records against all visible columns. You can also edit or sort columns. To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order.  To edit information about a device, click Edit  in the far right column.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
