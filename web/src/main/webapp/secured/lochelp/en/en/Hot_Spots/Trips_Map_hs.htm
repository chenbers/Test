This map displays your trips selected from the list above. As you click on a trip date, the trip displays on the map with start and stop locations, plus any violations incurred along the way.

 

You can use the slider to the left to zoom in and out. You can use the directional arrows to navigate up, down, left and right in the map. Also, you can click and drag the map itself to move the map.

 

The area you see on the map is what is highlighted in the inset by the blue square. You can move the blue square around to navigate as well.
