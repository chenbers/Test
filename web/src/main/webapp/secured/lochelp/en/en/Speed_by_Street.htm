Speed by Street

Our Speed by Street utility is how your device knows whether your driver is speeding. When you first click on the Speed by Street tab, you will see the initial view, as seen below.

You can submit a Speed by Street change request using this page. To do so, perform the following:

  1. Zoom in to the street for which you would like to submit a change request. The map will look similar to what you see below.

  1. Click on the desired street for which you would like to submit a change request.

26th Street was selected, clicked in between Alcott and Bryant Streets. Two options appeared: one for the section of 26th Street, and one for Zuni.

  1. In the New MPH column, enter the speed limit you believe to be correct for the location. Enter any notes for our Speed by Street team under the Comment column.

  2. When you have completed the section, you can select another street and follow the same process, or, if you are finished, click Submit Request.
