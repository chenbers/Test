Crash Details

Crash Details displays details about a selected crash or roll over.

Drag the blue car icon in the Crash Route map along the crash route and watch the graph below the map to see the speed and rpm of the vehicle along the route leading up to and following the crash.  The car icon with flames on the map and the red vertical line in the graph represents the point where the crash occurred. Details about each event surrounding the crash can be viewed in the Crash Events table in the lower left side of the screen.

To edit details about the crash click on the Edit button.
