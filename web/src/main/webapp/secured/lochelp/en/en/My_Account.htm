My Account

This page displays your personal information. From here, you can change your password or edit your personal information.

The initial screen will be similar to the image below. Your My Account screen might have populated information already.

To change your password, click Change Password in the top right corner. The following dialog appears:

You will need to enter your current password, then select a password between 6 and 12 characters. Your password can contain letters, numbers or special characters ONLY found on the numbers row, with the exception of + and =.

Once you have filled out the Current Password, New Password and Confirm New Password fields, click Save. If you do not want to save your changes, click Cancel. The Change Your Password dialog disappears and you are taken back to the My Account screen.

To change your personal information, click Edit. The following screen appears:

Fill out the information that you feel is applicable.
