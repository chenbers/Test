You can select a zone, name the alert profile, and add a description of the alert profile. You can also specify the days and time frame that the alert on the zone will be triggered. For example, if you select Monday (M) and 8:00 am to 5:00 pm and Report on Arrival, the alert will not be sent if one of the drivers in a selected group enters the zone at 5:05 pm.  If he enters a 4:55 pm it will be sent.  The time ranges are relative to the driver's time zone.
