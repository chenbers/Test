Zone Alerts

You can set up alerts for when a driver either leaves or enters a specific zone. This is a listing of all of the zone alerts in the system.  You can search for records against all visible columns. You can also edit or sort columns. To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order.  To edit information about a zone alert, click Edit  in the far right column.

From here, you can add a zone alert to your fleet by clicking Add Alert under the Zone Alerts tab.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.
