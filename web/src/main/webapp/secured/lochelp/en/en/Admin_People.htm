Users

This page is a listing of all individuals within the system. They can be set up as a driver, a user with portal login rights, or both.

You can search for records against all visible columns. You can also edit or sort columns. To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order. To edit information about a person, click Edit  in the far right column.

From here, you can add a person to your fleet by clicking Add User under the Users tab.

**NOTE**
When adding an individual to your system, you are not necessarily adding them to your fleet if you are only granting the individual portal login rights.
