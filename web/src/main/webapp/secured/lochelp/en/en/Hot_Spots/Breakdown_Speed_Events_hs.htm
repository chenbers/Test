The Details section lists the recorded speeding events. By default, columns are sorted by descending order (most recent first). To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order.

 

In addition you can:

  • Click an address in the Location column to see where an event occurred.

  • Click the Speed 55 icon to submit a Speed Limit Change Request.

  • If warranted, click 'exclude' to delete a recorded event.

 

**NOTE**

Upon clearing a recorded event, the overall speed score and their speed score for a particular posted speed limit range will not be immediately adjusted. The scores will be adjusted once the system performs a recalculation of such scores -- generally, once an hour.
