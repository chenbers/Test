Red Flags

This page displays a list of your fleet's red flag notifications. A "red flag" is any driving behavior that is considered to be unsafe: hard turn, hard brake, speeding, seat belt violation.  The criteria for what constitutes a red flag is defined by Administrators in the Admin section of the portal under the Red Flags tab.

You can quickly access detailed performance information about a driver or vehicle by clicking the different links on this page.

Red flags can be excluded by clicking on the exclude link at the far right of each row.

You can search for records against all visible columns. You can also edit or sort columns. To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.

 

Click the Edit Columns link to edit the columns that appear on the Red Flags table.
