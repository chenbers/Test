Drivers

This page displays a report about your drivers:

  • Group

  • Employee ID

  • Driver

  • Vehicle

  • Distance Driven

  • Overall, Speed, Style and Seat Belt scores.

You can quickly access detailed performance information about a group, driver, or vehicle by clicking the different links on this page.

You can search for records against all visible columns. You can also edit or sort columns. To sort columns, click on the top of the column. Clicking once will sort by ascending order. Clicking twice will sort by descending order.

To learn more about various areas of interest, move your mouse over the image below. When you see the icon, click that area. A pop-up window will appear.

Right below the Red Flags notifications, you'll see an Edit Columns link. Click this to edit the columns that appear on the Driver Report.
