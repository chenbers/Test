Overview

FusionCharts XT is a perfect addition to your reports, dashboards, surveys, monitors and analytics. Its comprehensive range of chart types with smart reporting capabilities, animations and interactivity are bound to make your apps look stunning and power-packed.

Leveraging JavaScript (HTML5) & Flash, FusionCharts XT functions seamlessly on PCs, Macs, iPads, iPhones and a majority of other mobile devices. It works with both XML and JSON data, and can integrate with any server-side technology (ASP, ASP.NET, PHP, JSP, ColdFusion, Ruby on Rails etc.) and database you use. With the extensive documentation and readymade demos it offers, you will need just 15 mins to create your first chart and get to your first "wow" moment. The stunning looks, refreshing animation and power-packed reporting capabilities of FusionCharts XT have enabled our 21,000 customers and 450,000 users to add "delight" to their applications.

Be it a simple column or pie chart, a combination chart, advanced zoom and scroll charts, or specialized sales and marketing charts, FusionCharts XT covers all your charting requirements. All the charts support interactive options like tooltips, drill-down, exporting as image, PDF, or CSV and JavaScript integration.

FusionCharts XT is a part of the FusionCharts Suite XT, but functions independently of the other products in the suite as well.
